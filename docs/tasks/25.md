# Objective

## Reorganize Controller Layout and Improve Timeline Information Hierarchy

---

## Role

You are a UI/UX designer. Approach this objective with the mindset, vocabulary, and priorities of a designer focused on usability, information architecture, visual hierarchy, and minimalist interface design.

---

## Description

Reorganize the application's controllers by categorizing them according to their intended purpose and placing each control within the most appropriate section. Evaluate the current tab-based organization and explore alternative navigation patterns that reduce the number of tabs or columns while preserving the same functionality and improving usability. Before making structural decisions, ask clarifying questions to understand the desired workflow and organizational preferences. Additionally, redesign the timeline section by improving its visual hierarchy, reducing clutter, and presenting information in a clearer, more organized manner without changing its purpose.

---

## Primary Objective

Create a cleaner, more intuitive interface by reorganizing controllers based on their purpose and improving the timeline's visual hierarchy while maintaining existing functionality.

---

## Supporting Tasks

### Controller Organization

* Review the current controller layout and identify controls that are placed in inappropriate tabs or sections.
* Reorganize controllers into logical categories based on their intended purpose.
* Evaluate alternatives to the current tab-heavy layout that reduce the number of tabs or columns while preserving usability and functionality.
* Ask clarifying questions before determining the final organizational structure to ensure it aligns with the intended workflow and user preferences.

### Timeline Redesign

* Analyze the current timeline layout for clutter and poor information hierarchy.
* Improve the visual hierarchy by organizing information according to importance.
* Reduce excessive text density and improve readability through a cleaner, more structured presentation.
* Preserve all existing functionality while making the timeline easier to scan and understand.

---

## Multi-Agent Feasibility Assessment

**Assessment:** **YES.** The controller reorganization and timeline redesign are independent design work streams that can be executed in parallel before being integrated into a unified interface.

**Parallelization Opportunities:**

* Audit and reorganize controllers into logical categories.
* Research and propose alternative navigation structures that reduce tabs or columns.
* Redesign the timeline with improved visual hierarchy and information organization.

**Dependencies to Manage:**

* Ensure the proposed navigation structure accommodates the reorganized controllers.
* Maintain consistent design patterns, spacing, and visual language across both interface components.
* Validate that all existing functionality remains accessible after integration.

**Named Sub-Agent Assignments:**

* **Information Architecture Agent** (UI Organization Specialist): Audit existing controllers, categorize them by purpose, and recommend a revised organizational structure.
* **Navigation Design Agent** (Interaction Designer): Explore and propose minimal navigation patterns that reduce the number of tabs or columns while preserving usability, and prepare clarifying questions for the preferred approach.
* **Timeline UX Agent** (Visual Hierarchy Specialist): Redesign the timeline by improving information hierarchy, reducing clutter, and increasing readability.

---

## Detailed Breakdown

### Controller Information Architecture

Review the current placement of all controllers and reorganize them into categories that accurately reflect their intended purpose. Eliminate misplaced controls and simplify navigation by exploring alternatives to the existing tab-heavy layout. Before finalizing the design, gather clarification regarding the preferred workflow and organizational expectations.

### Navigation Simplification

Reduce interface complexity by identifying more minimal organizational patterns than using numerous tabs or columns. Any proposed solution must retain the existing functionality while improving discoverability and overall usability.

### Timeline Information Hierarchy

Redesign the timeline so that information is presented with a clear visual hierarchy rather than appearing as uniformly styled, scattered text. Organize content to improve readability, reduce clutter, and make important information easier to identify without removing or altering its purpose.
