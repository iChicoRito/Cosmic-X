# Objective
## Enhance Laser Beam Visual Effects and Animation Quality

---

## Description

Improve the visual quality and responsiveness of the current laser mode for both Tap and Cursor Laser Mode. Enhance the existing effects and styling by making the laser appear more dynamic and visually engaging while preserving its current functionality. Refine the beam's behavior so it reacts more naturally to movement, especially during rapid motion. Also, improve the emitter-to-target growth animation so the beam visibly extends toward its target instead of appearing to activate almost instantly.

---

## Primary Objective

Enhance the laser beam's visual effects and animations to create a smoother, more dynamic, and visually satisfying experience.

---

## Supporting Tasks

### Visual Effects
- Add thunderbolt or lightning effects surrounding the laser beam while the laser is active.
- Ensure the additional effects complement the existing laser styling and enhance its visual impact.

### Beam Behavior
- Make the laser beam bend or curve naturally when it is moved rapidly, creating a more responsive and dynamic appearance.

### Animation Improvements
- Refine the emitter-to-target growth animation so the beam visibly extends from the emitter toward the target.
- Reduce the perception of the beam appearing instantly by making its growth animation more noticeable and smoothly animated.
