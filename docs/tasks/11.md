# Objective

## Expand Galaxy Exploration with Customizable Laser Interactions, Triangulum Galaxy, and Zodiac Visualizations

---

## Role

You are a game developer. Approach this objective with the mindset, vocabulary, and priorities of a game developer focused on implementing interactive gameplay features, realistic space environments, and maintaining system stability.

---

## Description

Enhance the existing galaxy exploration experience by introducing interactive laser mechanics, a new explorable galaxy, and additional visual elements related to zodiac constellations. Allow users to customize and use laser beams on planets while preserving the existing interface, controls, and overall performance. Expand the explorable universe by adding the Triangulum Galaxy (M33) with its own unique visual identity and educational content. Improve visual immersion by introducing randomized zodiac constellation displays within galaxies without affecting the existing user experience.

---

## Primary Objective

Expand the galaxy exploration system by adding customizable laser interactions, the Triangulum Galaxy (M33), and randomized zodiac constellation visualizations while preserving the existing UI, controls, and application performance.

---

## Secondary Objectives

* Implement a customizable laser system that allows users to shoot laser beams at planets.
* Provide laser customization options, including size, color, destructive capability, and other configurable properties.
* Add the Triangulum Galaxy (M33) as a fully explorable galaxy with a visually distinct appearance.
* Include realistic spiral arms, billions of stars, nebulae, and the NGC 604 star-forming region within the Triangulum Galaxy.
* Allow users to freely navigate the Triangulum Galaxy and discover procedurally generated star systems.
* Provide educational information panels explaining the structure of the Triangulum Galaxy.
* Introduce zodiac constellation visualizations that may appear throughout galaxies.
* Randomly display zodiac constellations with a 50% chance for each galaxy, selecting different zodiac constellations at random.
* Ensure all development is performed in a separate worktree to protect the stability and safety of the current system.
